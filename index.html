About Me & My Philosophy

Hi, I'm ASTU

I am Bhushan Patil, known as ASTU, a name that embodies my connection to nature and my purpose of being present with the earth. Over the last eight years, I have dedicated myselfto mastering the art of natural building through hands-on practice, community collaboration, and a commitment to sustainability.

As a traveler, I have explored diverse cultures, learning skills and traditions that shape my work. My journey has taken me across India and Indonesia, where I deepened my knowledge of bamboo construction and its potential in sustainable architecture.

My passion lies in creating structures and spaces that are not just environmentally friendly but also foster a sense of community, learning, and harmony with nature.

My Approach:

  • Eco-Conscious Material Sourcing
  • Traditional Craftsmanship, Modern Standards
  • Community training
  • Collaborative & Client-Focused Design
  • Building Healthy, Breathable Living Spaces
  • Passion for Sustainable Futures
